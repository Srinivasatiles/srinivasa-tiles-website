Our Heritage

Nearly a century of clay craftsmanship.

Our family's journey in clay roofing tile manufacturing began in the 1930s, establishing a tradition of excellence passed down through generations. That legacy was formalised with the founding of Sri Srinivasa Tiles, Nuzvid, Andhra Pradesh in 1990, a name long trusted for high-quality clay roofing tiles. Srinivasa Tiles (STH) was founded in 2013 as a subsidiary to extend that legacy into the growing markets of Tamil Nadu and Karnataka. Today, we combine generations of traditional knowledge with modern manufacturing technology to produce premium clay roofing tiles, ceiling tiles and jollies — trusted by architects, builders, dealers and homeowners alike.
